Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information that is received and collected by our Air Quality Monitoring Dashboard and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

We collect non-personal information automatically when you visit our site, including your browser type, language preferences, referring pages, and the date and time of each visitor request.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the purpose of providing you with accurate, real-time air quality data and monitoring. We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information as no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance the user experience. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device to remember your preferences or provide session information. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on this page. Please check this page periodically for updates.
